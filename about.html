About Us
Welcome to the Push Swap Visualizer, a tool meticulously crafted by Saad Loukili to aid students and enthusiasts in understanding and optimizing sorting algorithms in a visually engaging way. This visualizer is designed to bring to life the intricate operations involved in the 42 push swap project, a challenging and educational exercise in algorithm efficiency and data structure manipulation.
